# De-identified clinical protocol excerpts (demo only)

**Disclaimer:** Not medical advice. For internal staff training and workflow demos only. Consult a licensed clinician for patient care decisions.

## Protocol A — Adult hydration (mild dehydration)

- Encourage oral fluids: water or oral rehydration solution
- Monitor urine color and vital signs every 4 hours
- Escalate to Protocol B if no improvement in 24 hours or worsening symptoms

## Protocol B — Escalation (symptoms persist > 48 hours)

- Notify on-call physician
- Document intake/output and symptom timeline
- Do not discharge without physician sign-off

## Protocol C — Documentation requirements

All triage notes must include: time of assessment, protocol referenced, and patient identifiers per site policy.
