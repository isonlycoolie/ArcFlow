## Policies

### Late submissions

Each student receives **two 48-hour late tokens** per term. Applying a token extends the deadline by 48 hours with no penalty. After tokens are exhausted, late work receives 10% per day, max 3 days.

Problem sets must be submitted via the course portal. Email submissions are not accepted.

### Academic integrity

Collaboration on problem sets is allowed at the "whiteboard level" — discuss approaches, write your own code. Copy-pasting solutions is prohibited.

### Final project

Teams of exactly **three** students. One team lead submits the repository link. All members receive the same grade. Individual exceptions require instructor approval before Week 9.
